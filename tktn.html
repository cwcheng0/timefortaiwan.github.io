淡蘭古道北路 Tamsui Kavalan Historical and Cultural Trails, north trail (北路,小路行程; north trail, only a few hour walk)

主題:文化旅遊 Subject: Culture Tour

淡蘭古道北路跟中路的起點位於基隆暖暖，我們的步行起點，從暖暖的暖東峽谷出發，結束時也在暖東峽谷。對於企業包團，行程可結合附近地方共2個景點。 Culture Tours: Tamsui Kavalan Historical and Cultural Trails(North and Middle) starts from Nuannuan, Keelung. We start our trail from Nuandong Valley. If you book our tour, we can arrange another place to have a one day tour.

早上我們集合後，就搭乘旅遊巴士到暖東峽谷。在這裡開始我們的步行，加上沿途拍照預計總時間約2~3小時，午餐時間約1.5小時。 中午在大菁休閒農場用餐。 We get together in the morning. Then we take a bus to the Nuandong Valley. Including the light meal, the total trip is about 3 ~ 4.5 hours. We have a lunch in the Strobilanthes Cusia Farm.

結束的地點也在暖東峽谷。之後搭巴士回到出發點或到第2地點繼續遊程。 Our trip ends at the Nuandong Valley. Then we take a bus back to the starting place or continue to the next trip.

廁所使用：只有入口處無障礙廁所，全程有些地方有廁所，需步行上下樓梯。 WC: accessible toilets are available only in the entrance.


集合地: 台北市、新北市、基隆市或包團約定地點 Place to meet: appointed by tourists in Taipei/New Taipei/Keelung.
