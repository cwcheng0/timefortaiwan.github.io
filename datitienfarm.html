大梯田花卉農場 Datitien Farm

主題:農場旅遊 Subject: Farm Tour

大梯田花卉農場，位於台北市陽明山上，山上用餐不易，此行程搭配午餐。依據季節不同，農場有不同的花，我們的圖片以6月的繡球花為例
Farm Tour: Datitien Farm is located in the Yangming Mountain; our trip includes a lunch. They have different flowers in different seasons. Our pictures use June's hydrangea.

早上我們在台北集合，就搭乘旅遊巴士到陽明山上，徒步旅遊，農場人員導覽，用完餐後上車。
We get tother in the morning. Then we take a tour bus to the Yangming Mountain; the farm will guide us. We take a bus home after the lunch.
