東勢林場遊 Dongshih Forest

主題:客家旅遊 Subject: Hakka Tour

東勢林場位於客家聚落附近，附近也有客家文化園區。對於企業包團，行程可能結合附近地方共2個景點：東勢林業文化園區 & 東勢客家文化園區。 Hakka Tour: Dongshih Forest is near one of the Hakka areas. If you are a company tour, we can add the forest area and Hakka Culture Area. We need to add extra fee.

早上我們在台中集合，就搭乘巴士到東勢林場。建議選擇天氣晴朗的好天氣，可以享受森林中的芬多精。 We get together in the morning in Taichung. Then we take a bus to the forest. It is recommend to travel in a sunny day to enjoy phytoncine.

行程結束後，我們在林場享用簡單午餐。餐後就到台中原來出發地解散。 After walking in the forest, we have a lunch in the forest restaurant. Then we take a bus back to Taichung. 企業包團可以結合附近的東豐自行車行程。 A company tour can include the Dongfong Bike ride. Fee is extra. (Bike Rental + etra hour)

廁所使用：林場建築物中有無障礙廁所。 WC: accessible toilets are available only in the forest building.
