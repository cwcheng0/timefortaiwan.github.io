Taiwan PoPo Travel Service Introduction


About Us

We are a both outbound and inbound company. Outbound is 90% of our business. The other 10% is domestic and inbound tours. We founded in 2017. however, we have more than 30 years of outbound experience.

Our clients are usually the same groups, which are groups of local communities. Most of them are senior people. These tourists are mostly join our tours at least twice each year.

These people go aboard few times a year. This year they may go Europe and Japan. The next year they may go China, Korea, Thailand or Australia.

Inbound services are only 5~10% of our total business. we get these services by chance. Local people or travel agents provide the information to us. They are usually their relatives or friends.

Our tourists like a little bit hiking, eating, and sight seeing. Most tours are tailer made.

Our tours to Thailand are about 5 tours. Sometimes we ask Taiwan travel agents to help us with the tours. (They have a good price.)
